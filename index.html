What is Sexual Compass?

Sexual Compass is a sexual compass test that was first proposted by TBG. You will be presented with a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a sexual compass.
